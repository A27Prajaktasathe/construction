Welcome to Global Builder and Developer

Kangralkar Associates was established in 1987 by Mr. Shridhar Kangralkar BE (civil) in pune and has completed more than 35 years successfully in the construction field. It is one of the largest organizations in South Maharashtra. With numerous accolades to its credit, it follows all essential values and is recognized for the same.

The Company's administration includes highly qualified professionals who are completely involved in achieving the best for the investor by following all required goals and standards. Kangralkar Associates has always strived hard to keep its commitments and thus enjoys an extremely resonant reputation in the Construction Industry. With an earnest effort to provide a dream house to the desirous, it has to its credit 3.5 million square feet of construction that include over 5000 dwelling units and an appreciable quantity of Industrial and Commercial Construction. The Company constructs homes, shops, offices, schools, public places, and other infrastructures in and around Pune, Satara, and South and West Maharashtra. An ISO -9001:2008 certified real estate Company, it is continuously and steadily growing with an annual turnover of above 105 Crores.

Kangralkar Associates is steadily growing and has also entered into fields of infrastructural projects and especially into the construction and revamping/repair of precast arch bridges. The company has an active research and development department, a material testing laboratory, and an active RMC plant and adapts to the latest technologically advanced systems. One of its outdoor facilities, Devika Mangal Karyalay, is the most popular place identified for many multifare functions.

In 2014, Kangralkar Associates was awarded the first position in the Universal Award for Outstanding Performance in Construction in Pune for constructing a ladies' hostel in BMCC, Pune. That is one of the many honors the Company has received.

Watching a city grow in front of our eyes is an achievement as well as a memorable experience. Since its inception, Global Group has witnessed the steps of development across Navi Mumbai cityscape. While this is the smallest part of the picture, the city has been in the hemisphere of real estate development for over 14 years. Marking its presence across the urban skyline of Navi Mumbai, Pushpak Nagar, Karanjade, Kalamboli, Kamothe, Ulwe, with more than _____ projects both residential as well as commercial, the sole aim has been to create benchmarks with consistent development in customer satisfaction and value-addition. In the coming years, Global Group is poised to add many more landmarks of excellence to the already existing array of illustrious projects while simultaneously raising the bar of quality, innovation and progressive outlook.
